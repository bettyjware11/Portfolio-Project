About Me

I was born and raised in Memphis, TN. I grew up on the South side of town and graduated from Whitehaven High School. During those days, I was really into Spelling, Math and Art. I loved art so much that I took advanced placement art in the 12th grade and competed in competitions. I did a variety of things from oil painting to charcoal drawings. I even got into air brushing design for tee shirts and other items that people wanted to add that personal touch to. I received college credit for this art class and went to the University of Memphis on a scholarship. Once there, however, my interests turned to a more practical course and I pursued Accounting as a major. I did this for two reasons. I loved Math and I was convinced by those around me that I could not make money in the field of Art. I was told that I needed to find another subject that I could actually get a job with. This is what I did. I graduated with a Bachelors and went to work. I stayed in the field of Accounting for over a decade before I finally became burnt out with the political nature of the job I had. I knew it was time to get back to my artistic roots; perhaps even merging my Accounting skills with my artistic nature to build a platform...
